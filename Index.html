How Solar Energy Works

Understanding your solar system is the first step. Here's how sunlight becomes electricity that powers your home or business.

☀️

Sunlight

The sun radiates free energy every day — your system captures it.

🔲

Solar Panels

Panels mounted on your roof convert sunlight into DC electricity.

⚡

Inverter

The inverter converts DC to AC electricity your appliances can use.

🔋

Battery Storage

Excess power is stored in batteries to power you at night or during outages.

🏠

Your Home

Clean electricity flows to your lights, appliances, and devices.

1
Solar Panels

Mounted on your roof, panels capture sunlight and produce DC electricity throughout the day.

2
Inverter

Converts DC power from panels into AC electricity your home appliances can use. Also draws from the grid as backup.

3
Battery Storage

Stores excess solar energy so your home stays powered at night or during NEPA outages.

4
Your Appliances

Clean electricity powers your lights, fans, TV, fridge, and more — day and night.

🔴
Heavy Load Appliances
High wattage — needs larger system
❄️ Air Conditioner (1-2HP)

🔧 Industrial Machines
🌡️ Water Heater
🟡
Medium Load Appliances

📺 Television (32–55")
